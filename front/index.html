Why Programming

JoyofAdventure was set up to enable the training of teachers and the leading of DofE expeditions for local schools. It is an approved activity provider (AAP) of the DofE Award and has a Health and Safety Executive (HSE) Adventure Activity Licence (AAL).

JonathanoyofAdventure believes in the benefits to young people of learning in the outdoors and is passionate about equipping and training teachers and students so that they are able to competently and safely work in the outdoors, particularly through the DofE award scheme.
